How does the process work?

Step 1 - Get Cash Offer

Within 2 business days after completing our Cash Offer Form, you will receive an email from us that will include:

  • Our cash offer, valid for 30 days.
  • A FREE prepaid shipping label.
  • Shipping instructions.
Get Cash Offer

Step 2 - Ship Your Equipment

Shipping insurance is included at no additional cost to you. Your equipment will be insured for 100% of your cash offer quote. To meet shipping insurance eligibility, packages must be shipped with 3-4 inches of padding on all sides. A secure, tightly packed box with no loose items will help prevent any damage to the equipment during shipment. CLICK HERE to view our detailed shipping instructions.

Step 3 - Get Paid

Once we receive your equipment as described, we will process your payment in 1-2 business days. Get paid electronically via PayPal, or request a check in the mail. Checks may be expedited upon request for an additional cost.

How much is my equipment worth?

Simply fill out our Cash Offer Quote Form (found HERE) to find out how much cash we can offer. Our quote form will ask for basic info about your equipment, such as make, model and condition.

How would I get paid?

You can choose to get paid electronically via PayPal, or request a check in the mail. Payments are processed 1-2 business days after we receive your equipment as described. Checks may be expedited upon request for an additional cost.

How long is my cash offer quote good for?

Once you receive your cash offer quote, it is valid for 30 days. We want to make sure you enough time to finalize your decision and send us your device. If you need more time, contact us for an extension.

How does shipping work?

We will provide you with a FREE prepaid shipping label and packaging instructions. Shipping insurance is included at no additional cost to you. Your equipment will be insured for 100% of your cash offer quote. To meet shipping insurance eligibility, packages must be shipped with 3-4 inches of padding on all sides. A secure, tightly packed box with no loose items will help prevent any damage to the equipment during shipment. CLICK HERE to view our detailed shipping instructions.

How should I pack my item for shipping?

CLICK HERE to view our shipping instructions PDF. To meet shipping insurance eligibility, packages must be shipped with 3-4 inches of padding on all sides. A secure, tightly packed box with no loose items will help prevent any damage to the equipment during shipment.

How long is my shipping label good for?

Fedex will accept your shipping label up to 30 days after the ship date listed on the label.

What happens if my equipment gets damaged when I ship it?

All shipments are fully insured via Fedex. If your equipment arrives broken or with major damage that you were not aware of, we will file a shipping insurance claim on your behalf and do everything we can to get you paid in FULL. CashForCameras will review how the item was packaged and will solely determine if your claim is eligible for payment. If your item was properly packaged, then the shipping insurance claim will be approved, and you will be paid in full for your original cash offer.

What happens if the equipment I am selling is determined to be “not as described” by CashForCameras?

If your equipment is determined to be "not as described”, we will email you and tell you how it differs from your description. We will give you an updated quote based upon the actual condition. We will even include photos of any damage. Our costumers normally agree with our revised quote as we always strive to be absolutely fair. If you are not satisfied, we can return the item to you at no additional cost.
