Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work?

  1. Fill out our Free Cash Offer Quote form HERE.
  2. Within 2 business days you will receive an email from CashForCameras that will include:
    • Our cash offer.
    • A link to lock your offer for 30 days.
    • A link to print your FREE shipping label.
    • Packaging instructions.
  3. Using your FREE shipping label, ship your equipment within 30 days.
  4. Once we receive your equipment, you will receive payment within 2-5 business days.

How would I get paid?

Once we receive your equipment, you will receive payment within 2-5 business days. Get paid instantly and securely via PayPal, or request a check payment. Check payments are mailed quickly and are U.S. Bank verified business checks. Expedited check mailing options are available by request for additional cost.

How much is my equipment worth?

Fill out our Free Cash Offer form HERE to find out how much cash we can offer you for your equipment.

How does shipping work?

We will provide a FREE shipping label and packaging instructions.

How long is my cash offer good for?

Your quote is valid for 30 days after you receive it. If you need more time, contact us for an extension. At any point, you can cancel the sale and request that your equipment be returned to you. We'll even pay for return shipping!

How should I pack my item for shipping?

To meet shipping insurance eligibility, packages sent to CashForCameras must be shipped with 3-4" of padding on all sides, in a secure tightly packed box with no loose items that can rattle around and cause damage during shipping. CLICK HERE to view our shipping instructions.

What happens if my equipment gets damaged when I ship it?

All shipments are fully insured via Fedex. If your equipment arrives broken or with major damage that you were not aware of, we will file a shipping insurance claim on your behalf and do everything we can to get you paid in FULL. CashForCameras will review how the item was packaged and will solely determine if your claim is eligible for payment. If your item was properly packaged, then the shipping insurance claim will be approved, and you will be paid in full for your original cash offer.

What happens if the equipment I am selling is determined to be “not as described” by CashForCameras?

If your equipment is determined to be "not as described”, we will email you and tell you how it differs from your description. We will give you an updated quote based upon the actual condition. We will even include photos of any damage. Most of the time our customers agree with our revised quote as we always try to be fair. If you are not satisfied, then we can return the item to you at no additional cost.

Can I trust your website?

We have over 17 years of positive customer feedback and have paid out over $1.5 Million USDs to sellers like you from across America. Established on October 9th, 2002 on eBay, we've earned a prestigious 100% positive feedback rating over 5,000 positive customer reviews and 100% positive rating.
